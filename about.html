Understanding Zero Hunger

Sustainable Development Goal 2 is a call to end hunger, achieve food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture by 2030. Hunger and malnutrition remain barriers to sustainable development, creating a trap from which people cannot easily escape.

Learn about initiatives that support this goal or find ways to get involved in the fight against hunger.
